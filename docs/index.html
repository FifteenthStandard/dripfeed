Dripfeed

Dripfeed is an email-based application that helps you manage your reading list right from your inbox.

How it works

Send an email to app@dripfeed.email with a link to an article, post, or webpage that you want to read. We'll strip away the fluff and send you back just the content, straight into your inbox. From there, you can use your email client's native search, tagging, archiving, and forwarding capabilities to manage your reading list, and share and discuss with your friends.

Get started

No sign-up required, just send an email to app@dripfeed.email to get started.
